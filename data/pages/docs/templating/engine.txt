====== Tabulify - Templating Engine ======


====== Engine ======
A ''templating'' engine is the process that transform a [[templating|text template]] in a [[docs:resource:resource|data resource]] (ie document)

''Tabulify'' supports the following [[templating|templating]] engine :
  * [[native|native]] - the tabulify internal engine
  * [[thymeleaf]] - a natural templating engine
  * [[pebble]] - a python jinja templating syntax engine
