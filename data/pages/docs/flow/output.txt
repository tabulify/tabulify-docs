====== Output ======


===== About =====
''outputs'' are [[message|message data resources]] that a [[:docs:flow:step|step]] passes to the next/downstream [[:docs:flow:intermediate|intermediate step]] in a [[:docs:flow:pipeline|pipeline]]


===== Type =====

They are generally 3 types of output:
  * ''inputs'': the [[:docs:flow:input|input]]
  * ''targets'': the [[:docs:flow:target|target]]
  * ''results'': the result report
