====== Pipeline Input ======


===== About =====
''inputs'' are [[message|data resources]] that a [[:docs:flow:intermediate|intermediate operation]] receives:
  * from a [[:docs:flow:supplier|supplier]]
  * from a [[:docs:flow:intermediate|intermediate operation]] (ie the [[output|outputs]] of a ''upstream'' operation are the ''inputs'' of a ''downstream'' operation)
