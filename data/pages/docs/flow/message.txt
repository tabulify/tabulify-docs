====== Message Data Path ======


===== About =====
In a [[docs:flow:pipeline|pipeline]], the ''message'' that flows from [[docs:flow:step|step]] to [[docs:flow:step|step]] is:
  * called a ''data path'' 
  * and is a representation of a [[:docs:resource:resource|data resource]].
