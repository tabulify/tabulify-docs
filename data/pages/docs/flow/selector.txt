====== Data Resource - Selector ======


===== About =====
A ''selector'' is an expression that when matched will select [[..:resource:resource|resources]] before processing.

There is two kind of selectors:
  * [[..:flow:data_selector|data selector]] to select [[..:resource:content|data resources]] or [[..:resource:container|container resources]]
  * [[runtime_selector|runtime selector]] to select [[..:resource:resource|resources]] that are [[..:resource:runtime|executable]]
