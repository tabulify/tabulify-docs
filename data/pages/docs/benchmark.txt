====== Tabulify - Benchmark / Workload ======


===== About =====
''benchmark'' are data operations executed against a [[docs:system:system|system]] in order to measure its performance.






===== List =====


==== Tpcds ====

''Tabulify'' supports out-of-the-box the [[docs:system:tpcds|TPC-DS benchmark]].

==== Manual ====

You can also create your own benchmark with the following command:
  * [[docs:tabul:data:execute|data exec]] - to measure request performance
  * [[docs:tabul:data:transfer|data transfer]] (update, insert, merge, ...) - to measure request and transfer operation
